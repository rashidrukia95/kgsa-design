Our Classes & Physical Skills For 2024

KGSA provides a free, holistic high school education to 150 girls this year. The school emphasizes leadership and character development through extracurricular activities and employs university- trained teachers, setting it apart from other schools in Kibera.


The KGSA experience encourages soccer, after-school clubs, and life skills training. We partner with other organizations to provide mentors and leadership programs on a variety of important topics.


KGSA cares for the whole girl by providing sanitary pads, school tea and lunch, preventative and acute health care, and a social worker to support their mental health. The new dormitory provides three meals a day and a safe, stable learning environment. In 2023, KGSA is boarding 90 girls. We hope to expand to all students in the future.


KGSA and its partners provide scholarships for alumnae to pursue higher education. Depending on their high school final exam results, girls can qualify for a four year bachelor's program, a diploma degree, or vocational training. In 2022, KGSA launched a new alumnae association to better connect with our graduates.
